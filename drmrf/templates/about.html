SCHOOL BIOGRAPHY

The school started as Islamiyya School, situated at Warure Quarters in Gwale area. The School was established by Late Sheikh Malam Kamaludden Adamu Nama'aji Alfindiki in 1972. He gave the school its present name DARUL MA'ARIF (May his soul rest in perfect peace; may Allah reward him with Jannatul Firdaus ameen). He died 9 years after he named the school, in 1981.

After his death, his brother Malam Garba Adamu Nama'aji Alfindiki continued with this great work of his (Sheikh Malam Kamaludden Adamu Nama'aji Alfindiki). The school continues to exist under his leadership up to the time when late Malam Kamaludden's son Alhaji Shafi'u Kamaludden Adamu Nama'aji Alfindiki established a junior secondary school at the premises of DARUL MA'ARIF ISLAMIYYA in Warure Quarters both the school operates in the same premises.

The school has registered as Junior Secondary School by Kano State Ministry of Education in 2011, while in 2016 the Ministry of Education up lifted the leve of the school to Senior Islamic Secondary School. The school received its first principal (Mal. Idris Hussain) from Kano State Senior Secondary School Management Board (KSSSMB) in 2012.
Due to over population, the Management of school finally relocates the school to its present location Gadon Kaya behind Alaramma's house, by Layin Ubale Gwamnati.
The School has sections in the following subjects of studies:- Arabic Section, Commercial, Art and Science Section respectively, and occupied eleven (11) classes, operating both Junior and Senior Secondary with one mini library a multipurpose Science Laboratory, Examination office, Principal, Vice Principal and Proprietor's office respectively. In addition it has two staff rooms for both Male and Female staff as well as seven water system, toilet for students and three for Male and Female staffs, principal inclusive.

STAFF STRENGTH

The school employed the service of at least thirteen (13) teaching staff, eight (8) of them graduates in major subjects, (4) four staff from KSSSMB and two (2) teaching staff from Gwale LGA all graduates.
The student's population is getting to 300 both male and female. May Allah (S.W.T) guide us reward those who established the school, may Allah (S.W.T) reward Malam Kamaludden Alfindiki and Malam Garba Adamu Nama'aji and all that help us liftment of the school to its present grade with Jannatul Firdaus. Ameen.
